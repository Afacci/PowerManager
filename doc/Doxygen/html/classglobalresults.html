real(kind = prec) function globalresults::globfuelcost ( integer, dimension(0:ntime+1, nm), intent(in)  setPoint)

Calculates the costs to buy the fuel (in euro or any other currency according to the one used in the input)

where is the specific cost (per unit mass or volume) of the fuel for the 'th machine, is the fuel LHV (kJ per unit mass or volume), is the primary energy input. is the price in euro/kJ of electric energy for each client and is the time-step duration.
Note that even though international units are strongly suggested, any units of mass and/or volume is valid for and provided that coherence between the units of these two variables is respected. Moreover prices and LVSs may may be expressend in different units for differend machines.

Parameters
[in]cindex of the given set-point to be given as input. Defines the state of the plant
[in]ttime step index. Note t=x meas the x'th time step from the simulation start.
